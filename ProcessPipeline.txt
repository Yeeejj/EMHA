GOAL
Identify a person's emotion (HAPPY or SAD) from his/her handwriting and drawings
alone. The 400 answered FINALE assessments are used ONLY to create ground-truth
labels for training. At inference time, the trained model receives only scanned
handwriting/drawing images and outputs HAPPY or SAD. The precise thesis claim:
"predicting concurrent self-reported emotional state from offline scanned
handwriting and drawing." Primary contribution = the FINALE dataset and the
extraction pipeline; accuracy numbers are secondary (target 70-85 percent).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION A. NON-NEGOTIABLE DECISIONS (ENFORCE IN EVERY PHASE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.  Binary labels only: HAPPY or SAD. No NEUTRAL class. No exclusion band.
    All 400 respondents contribute to training.
2.  Finalized scoring scheme (24-item FINALE, Likert 1-5):
      Happiness items kept raw:      Q2 Q4 Q6 Q8 Q10 Q12 Q13 Q16 Q19 Q20 Q21 Q23
      Sadness items reverse-scored:  Q1 Q3 Q5 Q7 Q9 Q11 Q14 Q15 Q17 Q18 Q22 Q24
      reverse = 6 - raw
      adjusted_total = happiness_sum + (72 - sadness_sum)     range 24-120
      label = HAPPY if adjusted_total >= 72 else SAD          integer math only
      soft label P_happy = (mean_score - 1) / 4               stored, not used
      for the decision boundary
3.  Participant-level splitting only. Never split at file level (data leakage).
    All 24 crops of a respondent stay in the same split.
4.  skip_skew = True for all 4 drawing crops. Deskew ON for the 15 word cells
    and 5 cursive cells.
5.  PyTorch only. No TensorFlow anywhere (code, docs, or slides).
6.  ResNet18 pretrained backbone is the priority CNN. Activate
    use_pretrained = True in config.py (current default False is wrong).
7.  All hyperparameters live in dataclasses in src/utils/config.py.
    Modules run as python -m src.* from project root. black + flake8.
8.  Raw data at E:\EMHA_Thesis\DATASET\raw is READ-ONLY. All pipeline outputs
    are written elsewhere; raw scans are never modified.
9.  Self-report circularity is acknowledged openly in Chapter 6 and in the
    defense. It is scoped, not hidden.
10. Pre-register a minimum viable sample floor BEFORE looking at pilot data
    (recommended: commit to a floor of 240 usable respondents in writing).
